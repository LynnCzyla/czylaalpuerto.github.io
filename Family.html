Family Background

I come from a family of five, including my parents and younger sisters. My parents are both hard-working. My father is a welder, and my mother is a housewife who always takes care of us. Growing up, I lived with two of my uncles. One of them has a kid, which is my second-to-last cousin. On every occasion, my aunt usually goes to our house and visits us along with my cousins. We usually hang out and eat a lot. I grew up in a peaceful house here in Taytay, Rizal.

They've always guided me through my journey, and to who am I today. They have always taught me the importance of education, hard work, and perseverance. I am grateful for their love and support, and I know that I can always count on them.

I am grateful for what my family has taught me, and I believe they have made me a strong and resilient person.
